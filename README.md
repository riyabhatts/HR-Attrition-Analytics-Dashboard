# HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
# Project Overview
This repository contains a 2-Page Interactive Power BI Dashboard designed to analyze corporate employee attrition patterns and evaluate their financial impact. Utilizing an HR dataset of 1,473 employees, this project identifies key turnover drivers such as compensation structures, overtime culture, and role specific burnout and translates raw data into actionable HR preservation strategies.

# Business Problem
The organization is facing unmanaged employee turnover, leading to rising recruitment costs and operational disruptions. While HR tracks basic exit records, leadership lacks data visibility into the root causes of talent loss, resulting in three major challenges:

Hidden Financial Loss: The precise monetary impact of attrition isn't quantified, making it hard to justify the budget for retention programs.

Invisible Hotspots: HR cannot easily pinpoint which specific roles, salary tiers, or workload pressures (like heavy overtime) present the highest flight risks.

Reactive HR Policies: Management relies on exit interviews after talent leaves, rather than identifying early warning signs like low job satisfaction and poor work-life balance to prevent burnout.

# The objective of this project is to:
- Measure overall attrition rate
- Identify departments and job roles with high turnover
- Analyze the impact of overtime, salary, job satisfaction, and work-life balance
- Estimate financial losses caused by attrition
- Support data-driven HR decision-making

## *Major Findings:*
- Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition rates.
- Attrition is concentrated among employees in lower salary brackets.
- Poor work-life balance strongly correlates with employee exits.
- Sales Representatives exhibit the highest attrition risk under poor work-life balance conditions.

# Key Business Recommendations
- Reduce excessive overtime workload.
- Improve work-life balance initiatives.
- Review compensation for lower salary bands.
- Develop retention programs for Sales employees.
- Strengthen employee engagement among younger workforce segments.
- Project Outcome
- The dashboard enables HR stakeholders to identify attrition hotspots, understand workforce behavior, estimate financial impact, and make informed retention strategies using data-driven insights.
